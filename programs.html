LUBBA PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES

LUBBA has different semister programs and activities which it performs

All western new studends who are admitted at lira university either
on bachelors degree or masters degree should register with LUBBA.

LUBBA mother group has a subscription fee to all new entrants of Ugshs:10,000.
And a semister renewal fee of Ugshs:5,000.

following the definition of democracy, LUBBA as an association sat and decided to
engauge in democratic elections all all members have the rights to vote and choose
their respective leaders

The following are the available posts in LUBBA which members are entitled to contest for.

LUBBA PESA

LUBBA came up with an idea of Lubba pesa which has helped many students in the association and out of the association in that students are given loans which help them to clear their tution dues and settle their other out standing debts

How does Lubba pesa work?

Lubba Pesa allows members who have fully subscribed for Lubba mother group
to have different shares.

Each share is of Ugshs:2,000 and every member has full rights of having the shares that
you can manage

Payment is on a weekly basis and money is always submitted in on a sunday. A grace period of two days is given
for only monday and tuesday.If it exceeds, each share is put on a surcharge of 500 ugandan shillings.

About LUBBA loans

Lubba gives loans to all people with in and out side the association
These loans are given on the terms and conditions which are attached on the loan requisition form
