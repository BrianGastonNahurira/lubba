BackGround Of LUBBA

LUBBA is a registered association in Lira University Which unites together students who come from western Uganda especially the Banyankore and Bakyiga. LUBBA(Lira University Banyankore Bakyiga Association) was started in 2017 by Mackline Nimusiima who served as the Ist president of the association. It started as a small group of Three students and has successfully grown to more than 200 students. LUBBA works incorraboration with all other associations in the University.

OUR VISSION

To bring together unity and social empowerment, God fearing among the western student learners in Lira University and to curb their voices into a sounding and hearable one.

OUR MISSION

Bringing togetherness and collectiveness of western learners in Lango sub region Northern Uganda with other learners accross the country so that learners can feel secured in the land of Lango.

CORE VALUES

Living in a free sustainable and secure environment
Setting up a strong foundation for western basement
Unity among all western students in Lira University
God fearing community
Cultural empowerment
